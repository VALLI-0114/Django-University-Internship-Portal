Data Science Internship

Internship Overview

This internship provides practical experience in data science, including data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning. You'll work with tools like Python, pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and more to analyze large datasets and create visualizations and predictive models.

Duration

The internship lasts for 3 months, during which you will complete weekly tasks and a final project.

Cost

The internship is free of charge. Exceptional participants will receive a certificate and letter of recommendation upon successful completion.
