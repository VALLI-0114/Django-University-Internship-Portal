About JNTU GV University

The Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University-Gurajada, Vizianagaram (JNTU-GV) is a public university located in Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh. It was established on 12 January 2022. The university was named after Gurajada Apparao, a noted Indian playwright, dramatist, poet, and writer known for his works in Telugu theatre.

Campus

The University is spread across an area of 80 acres (32 hectares) in Dwarapudi panchayat of Vizianagaram district. The territorial jurisdiction of the university comprises three erstwhile north coastal districts of Andhra Pradesh: Visakhapatnam, Vizianagaram, and Srikakulam.

Engineering Departments

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Communications Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Metallurgical Engineering

Students are admitted into the undergraduate programs based on their score in the Engineering Agricultural and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Students are admitted into postgraduate programs based on their Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) scores and rankings or their ranking in the Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (PGECET) conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
